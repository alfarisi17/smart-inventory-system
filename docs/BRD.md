# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Introduction
This document outlines the business requirements for the Smart Inventory System, describing the functionalities and constraints needed to meet stakeholder needs.

## Objectives
- Improve inventory tracking accuracy.
- Automate reorder processes.
- Minimize inventory holding costs.

## Stakeholders
- Warehouse Managers
- Supply Chain Coordinators
- IT Support Team

## Requirements
1. Users must be able to view current inventory levels.
2. The system should send alerts for low stock items.
3. The application must generate reports on inventory trends.

## Conclusion
This BRD is fundamental to guiding the development of the Smart Inventory System.
